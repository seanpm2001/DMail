Update guidelines

As of November 12th 2019, new guidelines have been set for DMAIL updates. This was the first day out of the entirety of the development of SNU 3.0.5 where DMAIL received an update.

The new guidelines are that the client will jump in major versions each time DMAIL is updated in a branch of SNU development (for example, 3.0.4 development had version 1.01, the 3.0.5 version jumped to version 1.02)
